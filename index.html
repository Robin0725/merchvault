Privacy — the short version

MerchVault runs entirely in your browser. Your collection — every detail and every photo — is saved only on this device (in your browser's local storage). It is never uploaded, we have no account system, and we never see your data.

That also means: if you clear your browser data or switch devices, your collection doesn't follow automatically. Use ↓ CSV to keep your own backup.

We use a privacy-friendly visit counter (GoatCounter) that records page views without cookies or personal data.
